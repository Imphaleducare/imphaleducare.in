SERVICES

Recognition and Registration

The school was granted recognition by the Government of Manipur vide order No. 57 of 2017(No 46/13/2016-SE(S). The school also had already obtained Safety Certificates from Manipur Fire Service and Public Works Departments.

The school is registered under Manipur societies registration Act.1989(Manipur Act.1 of 1990)with an educational vision and clear missions.

Location

Kwakeithel bazar and Tera bazar with an
almost equidistance.

Admission procedure

The school is opened to all children irrespective of caste and creed.Students registered at the time of issue of admission form may get themselves admitted by depositing the annual fees and certificate in support of their date of birth.Other documents like transfer certificate and marks are not mandatory documents.

About the school

The Imphal Educare has been taking part in rendering pre school and primary education services to the children in the age group 2 to 14 years based on the modern educational theory and practices. It was established in 2014 in a serine area of kwakeithel konthoujam leikai/Tera Sapam Leirak of Imphal city. Its home like environment, child friendly learning activities of the school make the students comfortable in their learning, socialisation and also give them love for life long learning.
